BLOOD CAMP

Blood donations in India are conducted by several organizations and hospitals by organizing blood donation camps Donors can also visit blood banks in hospitals to donate blood or directly to a receiver. Despite the shortage of donated blood, efforts by the government and various organizations have led to a decrease in the demand and supply gap over the years The number of voluntary blood donors increased from 54.4% in 2006–2007 to 83.1% in 2011–2012, Blood donors in India donate around 350 millilitres of blood
